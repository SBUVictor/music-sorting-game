Shelf It Right

This version shifts from a quick demo toward mastery-based training. Each lesson teaches, shows a worked example, gives guided practice, then keeps serving similar problems until the learner passes.

What This Covers
Whole numbers, first cutters, later work-like elements such as R4, year, and later-line details such as SER.I, BD.20, PTS.1-6, FASC.3, ABT.5, REIHE B, T.2, and H.III:E2.
How This Version Teaches
Each lesson now includes a worked example, guided practice, a mastery target, error-specific feedback, and repeated similar problems until the learner passes that skill.
