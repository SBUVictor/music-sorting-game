Key Terms (Plain Definitions)

Whole number
The number after the class.
Example: M 2, M 2.3, M 3

First cutter
The first letter-number group after the class.
Example: .B103, .C4

Later work element
A short code after the cutter that identifies a specific work.
Example: R4

Year
The publication year.
Example: 1994

Later-line details
Extra information after the main parts.
Examples:
SER.I BD.20 PTS.1-6 FASC.3 ABT.5 REIHE B T.2 H.III:E2
