Shelf It Right

This version teaches music-score call numbers using the actual kinds of complicated labels you showed: decimals, cutters, Roman numerals, bands, fascicles, parts, sections, and work-style marks.

What This Covers
Whole numbers, first cutters, possible second-cutter or work-mark elements, year, and later-line details such as SER.I, BD.20, PTS.1-6, FASC.3, ABT.5, REIHE B, T.2, and H.III:E2.
How The Training Works
Each section teaches one layer of sorting, then tests it right away. The final activity asks you to place realistic music-score call numbers in proper order.
